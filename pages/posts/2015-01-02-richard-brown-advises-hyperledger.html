We're delighted to announce a new adviser to the Hyperledger team, joining Tim Swanson on our advisory board.

{% load markup %} {% filter markdown %} Richard Gendal Brown ----------- Richard is an Executive Architect for Banking and Financial Markets industry innovation at IBM UK, but joins our advisory board in a purely personal capacity. He's one of the world's leading authorities on crypto-ledger technology and maintains a widely-read blog on the topic. He is a mentor at London's StartupBootcamp accelerator to early stage FinTech startups and is a Chartered Engineer. We are delighted to kick off 2015 with such a fantastic addition to the team.
