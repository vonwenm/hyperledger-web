We're delighted to announce two new members to the hyperledger team, Tim Swanson as an advisor and Meher Roy as a contributor.

{% load markup %} {% filter markdown %} Tim Swanson ----------- Tim is a well respected thought leader in the crypto-ledger industry. He is the author of [*The Anatomy of a Money-like Informational Commodity: A Study of Bitcoin*](http://www.ofnumbers.com/the-anatomy/) and [*Great Chain of Numbers: A Guide to Smart Contracts, Smart Property, and Trustless Asset Management*](http://www.ofnumbers.com/the-guide/). He currently serves as the Head of Business Development at [Melotic](http://www.melotic.com), a curated Digital Asset Exchange. Tim is one of the most respected analysts in this space and we are delighted to have him on board. Meher Roy --------- Meher is the first independent contributor to the hyperledger project. He's an experience project manager and an expert in The Internet of Money - Implementing cryptographic money systems, cryptographic ledgers, and decentralised exchange protocols. In his spare time, he runs the Basel Bitcoin Meetup. He brings a lot of experience and new ideas to the team and will be instrumental in the development of our decentralised exchange mechanism. Expect to hear a lot of those ideas here on the hyperledger blog soon. We welcome them both to the team and look forward to working with them for a long time to come. Interested in joining the hyperledger team? [Get in touch](http://hyperledger.com/contact.html) {% endfilter %} {% endblock %}
